Signs You Need Storm Damage Cleanup in Encinitas

Catching these issues early helps you avoid costly problems and protect your property. Watch for these telltale signs that professional help is needed:

  • Trees or large branches have fallen on your property or structures
  • Broken branches are hanging in tree canopies, ready to fall
  • Trees are leaning at new angles after the storm
  • Large amounts of debris, branches, and leaves cover your yard
  • You can see exposed root systems where trees have been partially uprooted
  • Bark has been stripped from trees by wind, hail, or lightning
  • Trees that survived the storm are showing cracks in main trunks or branch unions

The Benefits of Professional Storm Damage Cleanup

Storm-damaged trees are among the most dangerous situations in tree work. Trees that appear to be resting on the ground may actually be under tremendous tension, ready to spring violently when cut. Branches hanging in the canopy can fall without warning. Partially uprooted trees can topple suddenly. These hazards require professional assessment and handling by crews trained in storm damage operations.

Professional storm damage cleanup also ensures that no additional damage is caused during the removal process. Removing a tree from a roof, for example, requires careful rigging and cutting techniques to prevent the tree's weight from causing further structural damage. Our crews are specifically trained for these sensitive operations and have the equipment to handle them safely.

How to Prevent Storm Damage Cleanup Problems

A little prevention goes a long way in avoiding expensive tree care emergencies. Follow these tips from our certified arborists:

  • Have trees professionally inspected and pruned before storm season each year
  • Remove dead trees and large dead branches that are most likely to fail in storms
  • Thin dense canopies to allow wind to pass through rather than pushing trees over
  • Consider cabling and bracing for trees with structural weaknesses you want to preserve
  • Document your trees and landscape with photos before storm season for insurance purposes
  • Know your insurance coverage for tree damage before you need to file a claim
  • Keep trees healthy with proper watering and care â€” healthy trees resist storms better
