Some municipalities in the Encinitas area require permits for removing certain trees, especially protected species or trees above a certain diameter. Our team handles all permit research and applications as part of our service.
Late fall through early spring (dormant season) is ideal for most tree species. However, dead or hazardous branches should be removed immediately regardless of season. Our arborists can advise on the best timing for your specific trees.
