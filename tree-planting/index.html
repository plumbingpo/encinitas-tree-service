Planting the right tree in the right location is one of the best investments you can make in your property. How to Prevent Tree Planting Problems

A little prevention goes a long way in avoiding expensive tree care emergencies. Follow these tips from our certified arborists:

  • Research your site conditions (soil, sun, space) before choosing a tree species
  • Never plant a tree deeper than the root flare â€” the flare should be visible at soil level
  • Water new trees deeply and regularly for the first 2-3 years during establishment
  • Apply 2-4 inches of organic mulch in a wide ring around the tree, keeping mulch off the trunk
  • Don't fertilize newly planted trees for the first year â€” let them focus on root development
  • Stake trees only if necessary and remove stakes after one growing season
  • Start structural pruning young trees within 2-3 years of planting to develop good form
