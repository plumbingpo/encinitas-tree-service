Tree Risk Management

Commercial Tree Risk Assessment and Liability Management

Commercial property owners have a duty of care to maintain safe conditions on their properties. Trees that pose a risk of failure can result in injuries, property damage, and costly lawsuits. Encinitas Tree Services provides professional tree risk assessments using ISA TRAQ methodology that evaluate every tree's structural integrity, health condition, and failure potential. Our written reports identify risks, assign priority levels, and recommend specific actions.

These assessments serve a dual purpose: they protect people and property by identifying hazards, and they demonstrate due diligence that can protect you legally if an incident occurs. Courts and insurance companies look favorably on property owners who maintain regular professional tree inspections and act on arborist recommendations. We recommend annual risk assessments for all commercial properties, with more frequent inspections for high-traffic areas and properties with large, mature trees.
