How to Prevent Land & Lot Clearing Problems

A little prevention goes a long way in avoiding expensive tree care emergencies. Follow these tips from our certified arborists:

  • Plan your clearing project well in advance to allow time for permits and scheduling
  • Identify and mark any trees you want to preserve before clearing begins
  • Have a surveyor mark property boundaries to avoid clearing beyond your property
  • Consider selective clearing rather than total clearing to preserve shade and privacy
  • Plan for erosion control after clearing â€” bare soil is vulnerable to washout
  • Check for underground utilities before any clearing that involves excavation
  • Get multiple estimates and ensure each includes the same scope of work for comparison
